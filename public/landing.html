How it works

Three taps. One real moment. No swipe fatigue.

DeepMatch trades photos and prompts for the thing that actually tells you if you click — a sixty-second face-to-face.

STEP 01

Spin to connect

Tap the glowing dial. Our matcher curates from people online right now who fit your vibe, intention and the in-between.

STEP 02

Talk for sixty seconds

Live video, no filters. An AI vibe meter reads laughter, pauses and eye contact — never showing scores, just sharpening the next match.

STEP 03

Keep it, or pass

Two hearts? Confetti, contact saved, conversation continues. One? They never know. No paper trail of awkward.

The vibe meter

An AI that reads the room — and only nudges, never scores.

DeepMatch listens to the shape of a conversation, not its content. We process signals on-device: cadence, mutual laughter, micro-expressions. It feeds into one quiet arc that shapes who you meet next.

  • On-device processing — your video never leaves your phone
  • No public scores, leaderboards, or rankings — ever
  • Get a private reflection at the end of every call
  • Opt out with a single toggle, no questions asked

Reflective matching

Your taste isn't a checklist. It moves.

After each call, DeepMatch asks one question — "how did that feel?" Three emoji, one tap. Over time it sketches a portrait of you you'd never have written yourself.

  • Three-tap reflection — feels like a journal, not a survey
  • Watch your "type" surprise you week by week
  • Toggle any signal off if it's not the version of you you want amplified
  • Export or delete your full profile anytime
