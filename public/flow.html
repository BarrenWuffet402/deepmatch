The Flow

Ten steps from stranger to someone who truly gets you.

Step 1

The intro video

Record a short video introducing yourself — no filters, no scripts. Just you, talking naturally. Think "first 60 seconds of a great date." The AI watches, listens, and starts building your profile from tone, energy, and the stuff you didn't even notice you said.

recorded once AI-analyzed profile seed
Step 2

The AI interview

Your personal AI avatar sits you down for a proper interview. "What's a green flag you never talk about?" "What do you actually do on a Sunday?" It asks the awkward things so your matches don't have to. Answers fill out your profile automatically.

AI-driven Q&A auto profile fill gets the real you
Step 3

Build your AI avatar

Generate an AI avatar that represents you — your vibe, your style, your presence. It's not a catfish; it's a translator. When someone wants to "get a feel" for you before committing to a call, they can chat with your avatar first. It speaks like you, because it trained on you.

trained on your voice pre-screening tool async dating
Step 4

The AI practice partner

Before real dates, practice with an AI girlfriend/boyfriend tuned to your type. Not to simulate a relationship — to surface your patterns. Do you deflect compliments? Go quiet when things get deep? The AI flags it gently. You show up to real dates a little more self-aware.

safe rehearsal space pattern detection no judgment
Step 5

Community profile roast

Share your draft profile with a small group of fellow users — not for dating, just for honest feedback. "This photo makes you look unapproachable." "This bio sounds like a LinkedIn post." Real people, zero romantic stakes, genuinely useful. Think peer editing, but funnier.

peer review non-romantic brutally helpful
Step 6

The live video call

A real-time video call with a real match. The AI sits quietly in the background transcribing and learning — not coaching mid-call. Afterward it gives you a private debrief: what topics lit you both up, where the energy dipped, what to follow up on. Dating with a co-pilot.

real human connection AI debrief after match training
Step 7

The compatibility snapshot

After your first few interactions, the AI generates a compatibility snapshot — not a score, but a story. "You both slow down when talking about family. You have opposite conflict styles but complementary communication patterns." Something to read together, talk about, and build on.
